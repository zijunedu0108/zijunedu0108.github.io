14.1 Infectious Diseases

Quick Review (要点复习)

Vector (媒介) Arthropods that carry pathogens (e.g., Aedes for Dengue).
Vertical (垂直传播) Mother to child via placenta or breast milk.
Herd Immunity (群体免疫) Occurs when 95% of population is vaccinated.

Paper 2 Focus (简答题重点)

Q: Why is vaccination more cost-effective than treatment? [cite: 119]

Show Explanation
Vaccination prevents the spread of pathogens, reducing the number of infected individuals. It is cheaper than hospitalizing severely ill patients during an outbreak.
