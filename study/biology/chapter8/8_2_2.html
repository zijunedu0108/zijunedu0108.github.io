Lung Volumes & Capacities

TV Tidal Volume

潮气量 (正常呼吸量)

Volume inhaled/exhaled during normal relaxed breath. (~500ml)

💡 Mnemonic: Tides flow in and out Very normally.

IRV Inspiratory Reserve

补吸气量 (额外深吸气)

Extra volume forcibly inhaled after normal inhalation. (2000-3000ml)

💡 Mnemonic: In-Reserve for Very deep breaths.

ERV Expiratory Reserve

补呼气量 (额外深呼气)

Extra volume forcibly exhaled after normal exhalation. (1000-1200ml)

💡 Mnemonic: Extra Relief Volume when pushing air out.

RV Residual Volume

余气量 (残气量)

Air remaining after forced exhalation. Prevents lung collapse.

💡 Mnemonic: Remains Visible in lungs forever (to keep alveoli open).

VC Vital Capacity

肺活量 (最大可用气量)

Max air exhaled after max inhalation.
VC = TV + IRV + ERV

💡 Mnemonic: Very Crucial for athletes (functional capacity).
